12. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Row-level security (RLS) policies on all database tables
  • JWT-based authentication with secure token storage on device
  • PII redaction in diagnostic breadcrumbs and truncated user IDs in backend logs
  • Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Use of reputable, security-certified third-party infrastructure providers (all EU-hosted)

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority as required by law.
